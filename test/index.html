The problem

It is not a secret that nowadays reproductive health is vital for us, especially for the young generation. Despite that fact, it could easily be ruined in case you are not competent in sexual education sphere, which is frequently happens because of lack of knowledge. Unfortunately, not every parent is able to competently provide their child with knowledge about sexual life, and in many countries this topic is absent from school educational plans.

According to WHO (World Health Organization) statistics, approximately 21 million pregnancies occur annually in developing countries among girls aged 15-19, and about 12 million of them end in childbirth. At least 777 thousand births in developing countries are reported to adolescent girls under 15 years of age.
Many people do not care about their sexual health, not because they do not want to, but because they simply do not know how to monitor it and what to avoid, what to preserve it
Many people are afraid to start a sexual life because of ignorance and fear, and this can negatively affect their health and self-esteem.
